How it works

Two separate controls

GPS GO / Pause Stop
  • GPS — turns location broadcasting on/off.
  • GO / Pause — drives along your route.
  • With GPS ON and a single point, the driver just stands there and broadcasts.

Building a route

  • Tap the map to drop points (A, B, C…). Drag a point to move it.
  • While driving you can tap the map to append a new point to the end of the route.

Map legend

  • A Start (A)
  • B Upcoming / editable point
  • Z Final point

Point options (long-press a point)

  • Wait N minutes — the driver stops here for the set time, then drives on automatically.
  • Wait until GO — the driver stops here and waits until you press GO.
  • Speed to here — set a custom km/h for the leg ending at this point.
  • Remove — delete the point.

GO ends any wait early

  • Pressing GO immediately resumes driving from any stop.

Edit anytime

  • Every point can be edited at any time — move it, change its dwell or speed, or remove it. Editing the route behind the driver keeps the current position steady.

End of route

  • At the last point the driver stops and keeps broadcasting that position.

Speed

  • Drag the speed slider any time, even while driving, to change the pace live.

Status label

  • Standing, Driving, Waiting, Waiting for GO, Arrived.

Phone setup

  • Mock GPS needs Route Spoofer selected as the mock location app.
