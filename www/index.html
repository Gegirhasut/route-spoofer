Route Spoofer needs two things before it can drive your device location.

Location & notification permissionAllow access so the service can run.

Developer options are off

Mock GPS needs Android Developer options enabled — a one-time setting. Turn it on, then come back and re-check.

  1. Open Settings → About phoneOn Samsung: → Software information.
  2. Tap Build number 7 times
  3. Enter your PIN/password if asked
  4. Come back here and tap Re-check
