Privacy Policy

Route Spoofer is an open-source (MIT) Android app for developers and QA to play a scripted GPS route while testing location-based apps. This policy covers the app and this website.

The app

  • No account, no sign-up.
  • Collects no personal data and sends nothing to any server. No analytics, no advertising, no third-party tracking SDKs.
  • The Location permission is used only on your device to emit a mock GPS location; your location is never stored remotely or transmitted.
  • The Notifications permission is used only to show the foreground-service status notification.
  • The route you draw is stored locally on your device; clear app data or uninstall to remove it.

This website

  • A static informational site. No sign-up, no contact forms, no personal data collected.
  • Your language choice is kept in your browser's localStorage so the site remembers it; it stays on your device and is never sent to us.
  • The hosting provider keeps standard server request logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp) for security and operation. This site is hosted by GitHub, Inc.

We do not sell or share data. As we collect no personal data, there is nothing on our side to access, correct, or delete; on-device data is fully under your control.
