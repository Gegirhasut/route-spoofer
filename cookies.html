Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses no tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies, and no third-party cookies.

This site sets no cookies at all.

It keeps one functional item in your browser's localStorage — your selected language ("route-spoofer.lang") — so your preferred language is shown on return. It stays on your device, is not sent to us, and you can clear it anytime in your browser settings.

The host keeps standard server logs as described in the Privacy Policy. Because no tracking technologies are used, there is no consent banner.
